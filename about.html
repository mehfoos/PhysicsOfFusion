Hello There!

This website presents information on some of the key concepts related to Nuclear Fusion. The focus is on topics relevant to realising a commercially feasible nuclear fusion reactor.

The content here aims to inspire readers to contribute to the field of nuclear fusion. While there are many excellent resources on these topics (some of which are heavily referenced here), this website presents topics to bridge the gap between popular science and academic coverage of the topic.

Another intent of this effort is a more personal one: to help me better understand the topics myself, by presenting them to others (Learning by teaching). For scientific or engineering challenges, I try to approach the concepts from first principles where I can, and then cover some branches for the “what-if”/“how about” scenarios, to explore alternate approaches to solving a problem.
