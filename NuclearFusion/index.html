Overview

Nuclear Fusion refers to a nuclear reaction where atomic nuclei are combined (hence “fusion”) to result in different nuclei, often accompanied by a release of energy when using lighter nuclei. This occurs in nature, and is the reason for our sun shining and for the synthesis of most of the elements.

The content of this website focuses on enabling humans to harness exothermic nuclear fusion reactions on earth for energy generation.
