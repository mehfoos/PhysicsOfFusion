Overview

Nuclear Fusion refers to a nuclear reaction where atomic nuclei are combined (hence “fusion”) to result in different nuclei, often accompanied by a release of energy when using lighter nuclei. This occurs in nature, and is the reason for our sun shining and for the synthesis of some of the elements.
