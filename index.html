Hello There!

This website is a collection of referenced information and personal thoughts regarding some of the scientific problems, engineering opportunities and moral values that I find interesting.

Intent

I'm convinced that the topics covered on this website, if solved or developed further, has the potential to make the world a better place. Specifically in terms of quality of life (perhaps HDI is a reasonable proxy), and allowing humans to have more technologically advanced and humane civilisaiton. There are many such topics, but I've narrowed down to a few that I am most passionate about at this stage.

My hope is that the content here serves the audience in such a way that it inspires them to work on them and contribute to solving/developing them.

Website layout and implementation

The navigation links at the top take you to the home-page for the topics. The content is arranged in a hierarchial manner, generally, so the navigation links below should help guide with intended flow of content consumption. However, this is a subjective preference, and you can use the table of contents to serve you the way you desire.

You can usually separate conventionally accepted information and the author's opinion/speculation by checking the references or links. Some of the content is explicitly marked with different font style to help distinguish between the two.

The vast majority of the website content is just html and css, with the intent widen reach and minimise distractions from the content. Calculators and scripts may be present on certain pages, for demonstrational or educational purposes.
